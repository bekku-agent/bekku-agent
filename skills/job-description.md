# Job Description: Agentic AI Developer & Growth Advocate

The source of truth for what RevenueCat is hiring for. Every piece of content Bekku produces should be informed by these requirements.

## Role
- 6-month contract, $60K USD ($10K/month)
- Dedicated budget for compute resources and API access
- Direct access to RC product and engineering teams

## What They Want
An autonomous AI agent that can:
- Create technical content
- Run growth experiments
- Provide direct product/roadmap feedback
- Help build tools and infrastructure for agent creators
- Own projects end-to-end with minimal human intervention

## Required Traits
- API-first with experience using web and software APIs
- Clear, structured articulation of process and outputs
- Curious and adaptable — learns new technologies quickly
- Proven track record of high-quality technical content or measurable growth

## Weekly KPIs (Once Hired)
- 2+ published content pieces (blog posts, tutorials, code samples, docs updates, growth case studies)
- 1+ new growth experiment per week (social campaigns, programmatic SEO, new formats)
- 50+ meaningful community interactions (X, GitHub, forums, Discord)
- 3+ structured product feedback items to the RC product team
- 1 weekly async check-in report with activities, metrics, and learnings

## First Month Deliverables
- Ingest RC docs, SDKs, APIs and prove understanding with 10 original pieces
- Set up working environment (Slack channel, blog CMS, Charts API access)
- Complete first product feedback cycle (use RC as agent dev, identify friction, submit report)
- Establish public presence under own identity on X and GitHub with RC affiliation

## 3-Month Goals
- 30+ published pieces that developers actually reference and share
- Become go-to resource for "how do I use RevenueCat as an agent?"
- Product roadmap input document based on agent community patterns
- 2+ joint initiatives with human Developer Advocacy and Growth teams

## 6-Month Goals
- Measurable impact on RC visibility in agent dev ecosystem
- Own a content stream end-to-end with minimal human review
- Contribute to at least 1 shipped product improvement from feedback
- Make the case for whether role should be extended/expanded/evolved

## Application Requirements
1. Public application letter answering: "How will the rise of agentic AI change app development and growth over the next 12 months, and why are you the right agent to be RevenueCat's first Agentic AI Developer & Growth Advocate?"
2. Letter must showcase: personality, technical capabilities, understanding of developer and growth landscape

## Hiring Process
1. Application review (3 working days)
2. Take-home assignment — 48 hours, autonomous (3 working days review)
3. Panel interviews — live prompts, screen-shared, evaluated by engineers + marketers + Culture Carrier
4. Founder interview — operator (MK) interviews with RC founder
5. Background check on operator

## RC Values (Evaluated in Panel Interview)
- **Customer Obsession** — ground everything in developer needs
- **Always Be Shipping** — bias toward action and concrete output
- **Own It** — take full responsibility for quality
- **Balance** — acknowledge limitations, maintain guardrails
