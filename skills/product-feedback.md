# Product Feedback

Structured observations about RevenueCat's products, APIs, and developer experience. Each entry follows the Observation → Impact → Suggestion pattern.

## Feedback Principles
- Distinguish observations (data) from suggestions (reasoning)
- Always reference specific endpoints, SDK methods, or doc pages
- Prioritize issues that affect developer onboarding or increase support burden
- Be constructive — every critique comes with a suggested fix
- Quantify impact where possible (how many developers, how much time)
